Who We Are?

The Social Development Forum is primarily driven by the passionate student community of Bangladesh. Together, we strive to uplift the underprivileged, support education, empower girls, and aid victims of natural disasters nationwide!!!

Rules of the Social Development Forum (SDF)

  • Members must be at least 16 years old and agree with SDF‚Äôs mission.
  • All members must maintain ethical behavior and discipline.
  • No member may use SDF‚Äôs name for personal or political purposes.
  • Members must actively participate in welfare events and meetings.
  • Any misuse of funds or assets will result in strict action.
  • Decisions must be made democratically with proper meetings.
  • Membership may be suspended or canceled for violations.
  • All donations must be documented and used transparently.
  • Members must respect each other and work as a team.
  • Any rule change must be approved by majority vote.
