Ethos and Values

At Social Development Forum, every member is expected to uphold the following values to ensure a united and impactful community:

  • Compassion & Empathy: Understand and genuinely care for the needs and struggles of the communities we serve.
  • Integrity & Honesty: Act with transparency and be accountable in all our activities.
  • Respect & Inclusivity: Value diversity and treat everyone with dignity and fairness.
  • Responsibility & Commitment: Take ownership and consistently contribute to the clubâ€™s mission.
  • Collaboration & Teamwork: Work harmoniously with fellow members towards common goals.
  • Continuous Learning & Improvement: Be open to feedback and always strive to improve.
  • Service Above Self: Prioritize the welfare of others with humility and selflessness.
  • Transparency & Communication: Maintain clear and respectful communication internally and externally.

Required Documents for Membership

To become a member of Social Development Forum, please prepare and submit the following documents:

  • Completed Membership Application Form (downloadable here)
  • Photocopy of National ID card or Birth Certificate
  • Passport-sized recent photograph (1 copy)

Please bring these documents to our office or any of our representatives in your area or send scanned copies via email during your application process.

Email: bdsdfcommunity@gmail.com

Documents Required to Become a Volunteer

To join as a volunteer of the Social Development Forum (SDF), please prepare and submit the following documents:

  • Recent Passport-size Photograph(2copies)
  • National ID (NID) (Photocopy or Scan)
  • Student ID (Photocopy or Scan)
  • Completed Volunteer Application Form (PDF below)
  • Proof of Address (e.g., utility bill or rent agreement)
  • Reference Letter (from a teacher, mentor, or community leader)
  • Statement of Purpose (brief paragraph on why you want to volunteer)
